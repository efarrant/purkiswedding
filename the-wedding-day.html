The Wedding Day
05.12.21

Welcome to our wedding website! We have built this to (hopefully) provide you with everything you need to know about our wedding day. This website is currently only in the early stages of development but we will continue to add more information as planning continues, so please do check back nearer the time. To try and keep our wedding as covid-safe as possible, we regret that we are only having a limited number of guests to our reception. But whether you are joining us for the full day or simply to see us wed, we will be so happy to see you there!

The ceremony...


The wedding ceremony is being held at Hertford United Reformed Church with the bride arriving at 1:30pm, and the service taken by the Reverend Martin Legg. Hertford URC was the childhood church of Emma and we are very blessed to be able to get married here. After the ceremony, photographs will be taken outside the church before we travel to the reception venue.
There will be hand sanitiser available on entry and exit from the church, and whilst we will not be wearing face masks during the ceremony, we more than understand if you would prefer to wear one.


Parking
Whilst you may use the URC's driveway to drop off those who require easy access, please do not park here as this area will be used for photos before and after the service. There are a number of places within 10 minutes walk of the church that you will be able to park, and more information on these locations will be added to the site shortly.

The reception...


The reception is being held at Bramfield Village Hall in the picturesque village of Bramfield in Hertford. There is step-free access to this venue, ground-floor toilets and onsite parking. Again, there will be hand sanitiser available at the entrance of the hall and we ask that you do use this for the safety of our guests.
